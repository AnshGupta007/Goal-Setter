about us

At Goal Setter, we believe that every dream is achievable with the right plan and commitment.

We make it simple. You set your purchase goal, and we calculate a manageable monthly installment plan tailored to your timeline — typically over the next year or two. Once you complete the installments, we help you acquire your dream item, hassle-free. No loans. No debt. Just smart, goal-oriented saving made easy and achievable. Join us and start your journey toward owning what you truly desire — one installment at a time.

FAQ

+

How much will it cost?

The total cost depends on the price of your selected goal and the saving duration you choose (e.g., 12 or 24 months). We calculate your monthly installment based on these details — with no hidden fees. What you see is what you save, and once your savings are complete, we help you purchase your goal item at the agreed cost. There are no interest charges or extra costs, just a small service fee included in your plan for handling and facilitating the purchase..

+

What is the duration we can put to calculate the installment?

You can choose a saving duration between 6 months to 24 months, depending on your goal and what works best for your budget. Our system will calculate the monthly installment based on the goal amount and selected time frame, giving you the flexibility to save at your own pace.

+

How do I track my progress?

You can track your goal progress in real-time through your dashboard, including payments made, amount remaining, and time left to reach your goal.

+

What happens if I miss a payment?

We understand that things happen. You'll receive reminders before each due date, and if you miss a payment, you'll have a grace period to catch up without penalties. Consistent delays may affect your goal timeline

+

Can I change my goal or plan later?

Yes, you can update your goal or adjust your saving plan duration before completing your installments. Just reach out to our support team for assistance.

+

When do I pay?

You'll make your first payment when you set your goal and start your plan. After that, payments are made monthly, on your chosen date. We'll send you reminders before each due date, and you can pay easily through our secure payment gateway. Once all installments are completed, we assist you in purchasing your goal item.
